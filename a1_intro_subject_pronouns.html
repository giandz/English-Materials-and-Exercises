Subject pronouns

We use a subject pronoun instead of a noun when we know who or what we are talking about.

The subject pronoun comes before the verb.
Use he for men and boys, she for women and girls.
Use it for animals and things.
Use they for groups of people, animals, or things.
Use you for one person or more than one person.
