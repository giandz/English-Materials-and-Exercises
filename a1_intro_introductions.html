Nice to meet you!

Introducing yourself and other people

😊 informal
🏫 first day at school — between students
👧
Ana
Hi! I'm Ana. What's your name?
👦
Ben
Hi Ana! I'm Ben. Nice to meet you!
👧
Ana
Nice to meet you too! Is that your friend?
👦
Ben
Yes! This is my friend, Sofia. She's in our class.
👩‍🦱
Sofia
Hey Ana! Good to meet you!
👧
Ana
Good to meet you too, Sofia! Where are you from?
👩‍🦱
Sofia
I'm from Mexico. And you?
👧
Ana
I'm from Brazil. Welcome to the class!
🤝 formal
🏢 first day at a new job — meeting a manager
👨‍💼
Mr. Park
Good morning. I'm David Park, the manager. And you are…?
👧
Ana
Good morning, Mr. Park. My name is Ana Souza. It's a pleasure to meet you.
👨‍💼
Mr. Park
The pleasure is mine, Ms Souza. Let me introduce you to a colleague. This is Dr. Maria Rivera. She is the head of the team.
👩‍⚕️
Dr. Rivera
How do you do, Ms Souza? Welcome to the team.
👧
Ana
How do you do, Dr. Rivera? I'm very happy to be here.
👨‍💼
Mr. Park
Ms Souza, do you know Mr. Ben Collins from the other office?
👧
Ana
No, I don't. It's nice to meet you, Mr. Collins.
👨‍💻
Mr. Collins
Likewise, Ms Souza. I hope you enjoy working here.
