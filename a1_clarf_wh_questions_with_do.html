grammar focus
What…?🎛️ options & categories
What sports do you play?
I play basketball and tennis.
What music does she listen to?
She listens to jazz.
What + category (sports, food, music, movies...)
Who…with?🧑‍🤝‍🧑 company
Who do you live with?
I live with my parents.
Who does he study with?
He studies with his classmates.
"with" stays at the end of the question
Where…?📍 location
Where do you work?
I work downtown.
Where does she buy groceries?
She buys groceries at the market.
Ask about a place
How often…?🗓️ frequency
How often do you exercise?
I exercise three times a week.
How often does he travel?
He travels twice a year.
Answer with a frequency expression, not a time
When…?🌄 general time
When do you start work?
I start work in the morning.
When does the semester end?
It ends in June.
Broad time — a season, month, or part of the day
What time…?⌚ specific time
What time do you wake up?
I wake up at 7am.
What time does the store open?
It opens at 9am.
Answer with a clock time
