A2 · Speaking Practice

Discussion — Past Habits

Talk with a partner using "used to" and "would". Ask a question, then ask a follow-up!

Before you start: take turns asking and answering. For every answer, try to add one extra detail — a place, a person, or a feeling.
Childhood
🧒 Growing up
  • 01What games did you use to play when you were little? Who did you play with?
  • 02Did you use to be scared of anything? Are you still scared of it?
  • 03What would your family do together on weekends?
School
🎒 School days
  • 04Did you use to like school? Why or why not?
  • 05What subject did you use to hate the most?
  • 06What would you and your friends do during recess?
Hobbies & free time
🎨 Interests
  • 07Did you use to collect anything? What happened to your collection?
  • 08What hobby did you use to have that you don't have anymore?
  • 09What would you do on a typical Saturday as a teenager?
Food & habits
🍽️ Everyday life
  • 10Did you use to like a food you now dislike, or the opposite?
  • 11What would your family cook or eat on special occasions?
  • 12Did you use to have a bedtime routine? What was it?
Looking back
💭 Reflection
  • 13What's a habit you used to have that you're glad you stopped?
  • 14Is there anything you used to do that you miss?
  • 15How were you different as a child compared to now?
