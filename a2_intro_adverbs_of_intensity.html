What's your city like?

A conversation combining adverbs of degree, opposite adjectives, and conjunctions.

✈️ two friends catching up after moving to new cities

Quick reference
and adds a similar idea. but and however introduce a contrast — but joins two clauses directly, while however usually starts a new clause after a comma or full stop.
