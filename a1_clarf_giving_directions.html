Direction phrases

Along
Walk along the river.
Go across / Cross
Cross the street at the crosswalk.
Turn around

Go past
Go past the bakery, then stop.
Go through

Location phrases

Next to

Between

On / At the corner
It's on the corner of 5th and Main.
It's on the left
The clinic is on the left.
It's on the right
The bakery is on the right.
Around the corner
It's just around the corner.
