Use these questions to practice talking about DIY and getting help with household tasks. Try to use have something done and get something done in your answers.

your habits
01
have / get
Are you a DIY person, or do you prefer to have things done for you? Give an example from your home.
e.g. I always get my shelves installed by a professional. I'd never do it myself!
02
get
What is something you have recently got fixed, cleaned, or repaired at home? What was the problem?
e.g. I got my oven cleaned last month — I'd never cleaned it myself before!
03
have
Have you ever had furniture delivered or installed? How was the experience?
e.g. I had a new sofa delivered but they put it in the wrong room!
decisions
04
have / get
When do you decide to do something yourself, and when do you get someone else to do it? What factors affect your decision?
Think about: time, money, skill, tools needed…
05
have / get
Which of these would you do yourself, and which would you have done professionally? A: painting a room  ·  B: fixing an electrical problem  ·  C: cleaning the windows  ·  D: replacing bathroom tiles.
e.g. I'd get the electrical work done by an electrician, but I'd paint the room myself.
06
get
Have you ever tried to do something yourself and then had to get it fixed by a professional anyway? What happened?
e.g. I tried to fix the fridge myself but I made it worse. I ended up getting it repaired by a technician.
opinions
07
have / get
In the conversation, B says "There's nothing like having everything done for you." Do you agree? Is it always better to have things done professionally?
Think about: cost, quality, personal satisfaction, learning new skills…
08
have
Some people have almost everything done for them — cleaning, cooking, gardening, repairs. Do you think this is a good way to live? Why or why not?
e.g. I think it's fine if you can afford it. I'd love to have my flat cleaned every week!
09
have / get
Is DIY popular in your country? Are there things that people always do themselves, and things they always have done by a professional?
e.g. In my country, most people get their plumbing done professionally, but many people paint their own homes.

to help you answer
