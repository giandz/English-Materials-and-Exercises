What do you do for fun?

Sofia and Greg talk about sports, music, and activities.

🏫 school hallway, lunchtime
🎛️ option 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 company 📍 location 🗓️ frequency 🌄 general time ⌚ specific time
Conversation
👧
Sofia
What sports do you play, Greg?
👦
Greg
I play basketball and swimming. What about you?
👧
Sofia
I play volleyball. Who do you play basketball with?
👦
Greg
I play with my cousins. Where do you play volleyball?
👧
Sofia
At the sports center near my house. How often do you go swimming?
👦
Greg
Twice a week. When do you usually practice volleyball?
👧
Sofia
In the evening, after school. What time do you finish swimming?
👦
Greg
Around 6pm. Hey, what music do you listen to?
👧
Sofia
I listen to pop and a little jazz. What kind of music does your sister like?
👦
Greg
She likes rock music. Who do you usually listen to music with?
👧
Sofia
Alone, mostly, but sometimes with my best friend. Where does your sister practice guitar?
👦
Greg
In her room. How often does she practice?
👧
Sofia
I'm not sure — maybe every day! When does your family usually have dinner?
👦
Greg
In the evening, of course. What time does your family eat?
👧
Sofia
Around 7:30. This was fun — let's talk more tomorrow!
Quick reference
Notice how the question word changes the whole answer: what asks about a category, who...with asks about people, where asks about a place, how often asks about repetition, when asks about a general time, and what time asks for an exact clock time.
