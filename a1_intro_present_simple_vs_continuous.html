A phone call

Ana calls Ben on a Saturday afternoon.

📞 Saturday, 3pm
present simple present continuous
Conversation
👩‍🦰
Ana
Hi Ben! What are you doing?
🧔‍♂️
Ben
Hi Ana! I am cooking lunch right now.
👩‍🦰
Ana
Oh nice! You don't usually cook on Saturdays.
🧔‍♂️
Ben
That's true. I usually eat at my mother's house, but today she is visiting my sister.
👩‍🦰
Ana
I see. What do you cook on a normal day?
🧔‍♂️
Ben
I don't cook a lot. I usually make eggs or a sandwich.
👩‍🦰
Ana
Ha! What are you making today, then?
🧔‍♂️
Ben
I am making soup and rice. What about you? What are you doing?
👩‍🦰
Ana
I am sitting in the park. I come here every Saturday.
🧔‍♂️
Ben
That's a nice routine! Do you read there too?
👩‍🦰
Ana
Yes, I always bring a book. Right now I am reading a new one about the ocean.
🧔‍♂️
Ben
Sounds relaxing! I don't read much, but I want to start.
👩‍🦰
Ana
You should! Okay, enjoy your soup. I am going home now, it is starting to rain.
🧔‍♂️
Ben
Bye Ana! Enjoy the rest of your day.
Quick reference
Present simple talks about routines and general facts (I usually eat...). Present continuous talks about actions happening right now (I am cooking...).
