quick reference: reported speech
●  original tense ●  backshifted tense ●  pronoun / possessive ●  time / place expression ●  linking word
Statement
👩‍🦱 "I will send you the report tomorrow."
→ She said (that) she would send me the report the next day.
Request
🧑 "Can you open my window?"
→ He asked her to open his window.
Command
👩‍🦳 "Don't touch my desk today,"
→ She told him not to touch her desk that day.
Yes-No Question
👧 "Are you coming tonight?"
→ She asked if / whether I was coming that night.

🧑 "Does the bus stop near your house?"
→ He asked if / whether the bus stopped near my house.
Wh-Question
👩‍🦳 "What time is the meeting tomorrow?"
→ She asked what time the meeting was the following day.

👴 "Where do you live now?"
→ He asked where I lived then.
