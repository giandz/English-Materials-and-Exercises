the three structures
for + verb-ing
purpose of a thing or action
I use my phone for checking emails.
This app is for storing passwords.
A laptop is useful for working remotely.
to + infinitive
purpose of an action or decision
I use my phone to check emails.
She bought a tablet to watch videos.
He opened the app to send a file.
by + verb-ing
how something is done — the method
I fixed it by restarting the device.
Save battery by turning off the screen.
She learned by watching tutorials.
for + ing vs to + infinitive — same meaning, different focus
for+ing I use Dropbox for storing files. (describes what the tool is used for)
to+inf I opened Dropbox to store a file. (describes why I did something)
by+ing I saved space by deleting old files. (describes the method used)
